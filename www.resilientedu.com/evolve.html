Inside the EVOLVE Curriculum

MODULE 1 Master The Fundamentals of Movement and Breathing

In the first week, you will be added to the EVOLVE Slack Group, where you will introduce yourself and meet your peers. We will discuss the importance of respiration as it relates to movement and cover the following topics:

  • Develop an understanding of dynamic systems theory and movement
  • Identify overarching principles and neurological influence of movement and breathing
  • Discuss how structure influences muscle activity

You’ll finish this week with a 1-hour small group Tutorial Zoom Call with Alex Effer. During this tutorial, he will go over all of the questions you may have around the fundamentals of movement and breathing.

MODULE 2 Build An Inside-Out Perspective Of The Thorax and Pelvis

The second week of the EVOLVE mentorship is all about how the shapes of the ribcage and pelvis influence movement. This week you will be given the opportunity to be place in a 3-4 person learning group to maximize your learning experience (participation is optional). We will also cover the following topics:

  • Identify the important anatomical features of the thorax and pelvis
  • How different reaching activities can influence mobility at the shoulder
  • How respiration and gait can influence movement
  • Pelvic floor anatomy
  • What thoracic rotation really is from an inside-out perspective

You’ll finish this week with a 1 hour small group Tutorial Zoom Call with Alex Effer. During this tutorial, he will go over all of the questions you may have around the Thorax and Pelvis.

MODULE 3 Uncover The Power of Gait and Foot Mechanics In Your Assessment

During the third week of EVOLVE, we will do a deep dive into the foot, gait and knee mechanics. This week is when everyone is encouraged to post postural pictures and gait for the group to assess. We will also cover the following key concepts: 

  • Discuss the two shapes of the foot during gait
  • Identifying how we can use specific aspects of the foot to understand what's going on at the pelvis
  • Determine how we can use gait to categorize exercises
  • How to assess the foot and knee position to create specific exercises to improve mobility

You’ll finish this week with a 1 hour small group Tutorial Zoom Call with Alex Effer. During this tutorial, he will go over all of the questions you may have around the foot and gait.

MODULE 4 Build An Assessment Model That Accelerates Client Results

During the fourth week of EVOLVE, we will break down some of the biggest misconceptions commonly seen in the traditional assessment practices. More importantly, we will be deep diving into the assessment model. We will also introduce the following key concepts:

  • Discuss the Resilient Hierarchy of Assessment
  • Utilize the 8 measurements to create a complete framework of movement limitations
  • Learn exactly what information the assessment tests are providing and how we can select exercises based on these measurements
  • Identify limitations during the assessment and why they are occurring 

You’ll finish this week with a 1 hour small group Tutorial Zoom Call with Alex Effer. During this tutorial, he will go over all of the questions you may have around assessment.

MODULE 5 Master The Assessment Model To Strategically Select Exercises

During the fifth week, we will dive deeper into the assessment model that further allows us to identify postural and movement limitations. This week we will be covering the following key concepts:

  • Discussing the two types of hip and shoulder internal rotation and how they are important for exercise selection
  • How hip/shoulder extension, internal/external rotation and abduction/adduction can tell us a lot about the position of the ribcage and pelvis
  • Integrating the assessment model from Week 4 and adding additional measurements to get a full postural and movement perspective to more strategically select exercises
MODULE 6 Gait Assessment and Practical Application

During the sixth week, we will explore what gait can tell you about movement and the compensations that you may see:

  • Discuss the phases of gait through analysis 
  • Determine how we can use a gait to identify limitations in movement
  • Learn important characteristics to consider for gait and movement 
  • Use case studies to reinforce the concepts discussed

You’ll finish this week with a 1 hour small group Tutorial Zoom Call with Alex Effer. During this tutorial, he will go over all of the questions you may have around gait and assessment.

MODULE 7 Develop An Exercise Selection Process To Make Programming Easy

During week seven, we will take a deep dive into the exercise selection process
and cover the following key concepts:

  • Develop an understanding of what specific exercises are actually doing
  • Categorizing exercises based on the assessment to make exercise selection seamless 
  • Learn how to manipulate tempo, speed, force, gravity, load etc, to improve fitness and mobility qualities
  • Efficient organization of a session to recapture movement and enhance fitness qualities

You’ll finish this week with a 1 hour small group Tutorial Zoom Call with Alex Effer. During this tutorial, he will go over all of the questions you may have around the exercise selection process.

MODULE 8 Advanced Programming Strategies

During week eight, we will identify how to use constraints and other tools to maximize results
and cover the following key concepts:

  • Identify how to select the most effective exercise at the appropriate time.
  • Develop advanced exercise selection tools to navigate complex movement limitations.
  • Discuss unique exercises based on principles to improve mobility 
  • How to modify weight room exercises based on movement limitations
  • Discuss the 3 primary Resilient principles that have the biggest impact on exercise selection

You’ll finish this week with a 1 hour small group Tutorial Zoom Call with Alex Effer. During this tutorial, he will go over all of the questions you may have around programming strategies.

MODULE 9 Application of Model To Case Studies

Week 9 is where we bring all of the biomechanics material together. We will select one volunteer to assess LIVE on the Tutorial call. During this assessment, we will put into practice all of the concepts and tools learn during the mentorship. We will also cover the following key concepts:

  • Use the assessment and exercise selection process on case studies
  • Introduction to exercise selection based on case studies and assessment measurements
  • Gain insight into how to apply these concepts into a live session

You’ll finish this week with a 2 hour small group Tutorial Zoom Call with Alex Effer. During this tutorial, he will asses an EVOLVE student LIVE and go over all of the questions you may have.

MODULE 10 Business Foundations

During the final week of EVOLVE, we shift focus to the business side things. This week is dedicated to helping you build something sustainable around the work you're already doing well. We'll cover the following key concepts:

  • Improving your social media presence as a movement professional
  • Strategies for client acquisition that align with the way you already work
  • Client retention- how to keep the clients you have and reduce drop-off

You'll finish this week with a 1-hour Tutorial Zoom Call with Alex Effer. During this tutorial, he will review real business situations from EVOLVE students and answer all of your questions about building and growing your practice.

What is the time commitment needed?

We recommend approximately 3 hours per week. There is a 60-90 minute pre-recorded presentation and a live Q&A every week that also consists of 60-90 minutes. We also have a list of guest speakers every week with presentations spanning from 45-70 minutes. As a reminder, you will have access to this material to go at your own pace!

Is the material recorded?

Yes! The weekly lectures are pre-recorded and posted every Monday for you to watch on your own time. The live weekly Q&A Tutorials are recorded and posted after the Zoom Call is over on the Resilient Learning Portal. You can rewatch the presentations as many times as you’d like at your own convenience.

What if I can't make the next scheduled EVOLVE?

If you are conflicted with time and can’t join the current mentorship then get started now with my self-paced Foundations Biomechanics Course. This course helps you get more from the EVOLVE mentorship and a great place to start before the next cohort.

What happens when the mentorship is finished?

We have created a continuing education platform called “The Vault”. The learning group is exclusive to the EVOLVE graduates. You will get the 1st month free for joining EVOLVE and gain access to all of the previous educational calls. More importantly, you will gain access to the EVOLVE tutorials (live and recorded), group discussions, discounts on products and direct access to me and a group of like-minded professionals!

How long will I have access to mentorship content?

You will have access to the course materials fo a total of 6 months starting on the first day of the mentorship. You are given an extra 9 weeks after the mentors is over to review the lecture videos or weekly tutorial recordings.

Yes, over the years we have has numerous clinics, gym staff and professional organizations run through the EVOLVE mentorship program using our group rate program.
