Revolutionize Your Posture from the Inside Out 

Traditional methods of improving posture often rely on stretching muscles, but The Resilient Posture Program takes a different approach. By influencing your body from the inside out, we address the true factors affecting your posture—joint positions and internal pressure.

 

Why Posture Matters

Posture is a reflection of how are body compensates to keep us upright against gravity. However, this compensation often leads to tension and discomfort. By targeting the root causes of poor posture, we can bring balance back to your body, improving mobility and reducing pain.

Program Overview

The Resilient Posture Program is designed to be convenient and effective, taking just 15 minutes a day over a 6-week period. The Program is divided into three progressive phases, each building on the last to enhance your posture and mobility.

Phase 1: Reposition

(Weeks 1-2)

In the first phase, we focus on recentering your body’s gravity. By targeting the base of the neck, lower ribcage, and the lower part of the hips/pelvis, we aim to reduce tension and bring your center of gravity back to its natural position.

START PHASE 1

Phase 2: Restore

(Weeks 3-4)

The second phase is about improving internal and external rotation, targeting the shoulders, hips, and ankles. This phase enhances mobility and flexibility, teaching you how to open up the front of the hips and chest for deeper flexion.

START PHASE 2

Phase 3: Range

(Weeks 5-6)

In the final phase, we focus on achieving deeper ranges of motion, specifically in the shoulders and hips. This phase expands your end ranges of motion, concentrating on both flexion and extension to promote comprehensive mobility.

START PHASE 3
